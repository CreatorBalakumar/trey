About Us

Hayagriva is an avatar of the god Vishnu and worshipped as the god of knowledge and wisdom. Taking Inspiration from that we’re constantly thinking about adapting our teaching approach to incorporate the ever-increasing, ever-changing students’ face as they grow.

To be a nurturing, innovative, and inclusive early childhood education center that inspires lifelong learning, creativity, and emotional well-being, empowering young minds to grow into compassionate, confident, and responsible global citizens.

Little Hayagriva Kids Zone is dedicated to providing a safe, supportive, and stimulating environment where children can explore, learn, and develop holistically. Through our carefully designed curriculum and skill monitoring system, we aim to foster each child’s cognitive, social, emotional, and physical growth. We are committed to engaging children in meaningful activities that encourage curiosity, cultural awareness, resilience, and a love for learning. Our mission is to collaborate with parents and the community to create an enriching foundation that prepares every child for a successful and fulfilling journey in education and life.
